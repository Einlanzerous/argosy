# Argosy — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-29_

Argosy is a client for a self-hosted media server. You connect the app to a
server that **you** run and control.

## Data we collect

None. The Argosy app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with
the developer or any third party. There are no analytics, advertising, or
tracking services in the app.

## Data stored on your device

To connect to your server, the app stores your server address and login/pairing
credentials locally in the device's encrypted storage (Android Keystore). This
information never leaves your device except to authenticate directly with the
server you configure.

## Network connections

The app communicates only with the self-hosted Argosy server address you provide.
Any data handled during use (your library, playback progress, profiles) lives on
your own server, governed by however you operate it — not by the developer.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.
